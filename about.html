Meet Kafruniverse

Venezuelan artist based in Miami

I am currently an art student who aspires to become a cartoon animator and a comic illustrator. I want to be able to influence people's day for the better. Many cartoons like Steven Universe by Rebecca Sugar, have helped me feel better when I have had a rough week. Some people underestimate the power of a heartwarming story. Human race has been able to communicate through art since the beginning of time, and I want to use my skills to contribute to making the world a calmer place

When my family moved to the United States, our lives took a complete turn, and I belive that we had to go through everything, so I could be where I am today.
