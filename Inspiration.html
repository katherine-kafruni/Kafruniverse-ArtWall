Inspiration

When I am planing an art work, I grab inspirations from many different artist, their themes, and how they tackle certain issues when completing their work. Like Igor Stravinsky said "A good composer does not imitate; he steals"

Social media artist

Loish

Loish is one of the biggest instagram artist that I have followed since I got an art account. Her art style is very unique, adn she always tries to help new artists learn some basic tips. She is recognized worldwide because of her stunning illustrations. I love how neat her color choice is.

Picolo

Picolo is one of the artist I look up to because of his original series called "Icarus and the Sun." He reached popularity on social media because of his series of fanart to the loved show "Teen Titans." After that, he got to illustrate two books, and more to come, about the show with the writer Kami Garcia.

Comic Artists

Hiro Mashima

I love reading webcomics, but there is something about a good manga that I cannot resist. Fairy Tail is a story based on characters of a wizard guild, who use their powers to protect each other. Most of the time they a little overboard and destroy the towns they are trying to protect. The love that each member holds for one another, warns my heart everytime.

Michael Danto DiMarino, Bryan Konietzo

Next to a few more creators, Michael Danto Dimarino and Bryan Konietzo created the masterpiece known as "Avtar, The Last Airbender" and the sequel known as "The Legend of Korra." Both stories touch bases on social issues, self reflection and growth, while remaining pg. Not to mention that the art style is delicious.

Korra's story also showcases- although not explicitely- a same-sex couple, before anyone else at the time. Yay inclusion!
